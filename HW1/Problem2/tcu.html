Pizza Snob

3051 S University Dr

There's no question I'll be back to try many more toppings and combinations - this place is awesome! About my only complaint is the fact that its proximity to TCU makes finding a place to park more than a little challenging. For pizza this good, challenge accepted!

Recommendation: Build your own pizza + an order of popcorn chicken.

4.

Fruitealicious

3043 Greene Ave

I am in love! Every time I go here it's always pleasant. Today however, I was greeted by Carmen and her cheery attitude rubbed off on me! Eli and Marco made my drink and it was so good. This is a very nice spot to grab boba and it's close to other restaurants and right around the corner from TCU. It's a nice place to get your boba to go or sit and read a book.

Recommendation: Thai Milk Tea
